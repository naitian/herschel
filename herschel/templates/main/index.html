what is blueprint?

Blueprint Literary Magazine is a student-produced literary magazine based in the University of Michigan's North Campus. It was founded in 2010 when a group of like-minded students from across the College of Engineering recognized the need for a medium through which students and faculty of the College could showcase their creativity in non-engineering subjects.

Since then, Blueprint has expanding on this founding principle to accept submissions from all memebers of the university community who are interested in supporting our mission of artistic diversity and creative expression, including students, faculty, and staff.
